PokéDex

Kanto

The Kanto region, the first introduced in the Pokémon series, features the original 151 Pokémon. Known for its diverse ecosystems, it includes cities like Pallet Town and Celadon City.

  • Bulbasaur
  • Charmander
  • Squirtle
  • Pikachu
  • Mewtwo

Johto

Johto, west of Kanto, introduces 100 new Pokémon. Its cultural blend of tradition and modernity is seen in places like Ecruteak City and the Whirl Islands.

  • Chikorita
  • Cyndaquil
  • Totodile
  • Togepi
  • Tyranitar

Hoenn

Hoenn, a tropical region, adds 135 Pokémon. Its vast oceans and volcanic landscapes define areas like Slateport City and Mt. Chimney.

  • Treecko
  • Torchic
  • Mudkip
  • Absol
  • Rayquaza
